Home Page - Game Functions - osu! Community - Different Play Styles

There are many different ways to play osu! The most common ways are mouse and keyboard or tablet and keyboard. There are other ways that are often unconvientional such as playing with a PlayStation controller or with a Guitar Hero guitar.

Mouse and Keyboard
is one of the most common ways to play osu! as the player doesn't need to go out and buy anything else compared against the other common playstyle, tablet and keyboard.
Tablet and Keyboard
is another common way to play

Among the different ways to play the base game, there are also the three other gamemodes that the game has;

  • Mania
  • Taiko
  • Catch the Beat, or CTB

These gamemodes are inspired off some of the game modes mentioned in the home page such as StepMania and Taiko No Tatsujin. Although these gamemodes aren't as popular as the base game, many players around the world still play these gamemodes and have their own leaderboards and ranking systems that differ from the main game.
