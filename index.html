Welcome to osu!

osu! is a free-to-play rhythm game developed and published by Dean "peppy" Herbert in September 16, 2007. Gameplay is inspired by other rhythm games such as Osu! Tatakea! Ouendan, Taiko no Tatsujin, and StepMania. The game has over 15,200,000 registered users.
